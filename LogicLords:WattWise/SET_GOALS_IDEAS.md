# Set Goals Feature Ideas for WattWise

## 1. **Energy Consumption Goals**
- **Monthly kWh Target**: Set a monthly energy consumption limit (e.g., "Use less than 250 kWh this month")
- **Daily Usage Goals**: Set daily consumption targets for specific appliances
- **Reduction Goals**: "Reduce energy consumption by 20% compared to last month"
- **Peak Hours Avoidance**: "Avoid using high-power appliances during peak hours (6-10 PM)"

## 2. **Cost-Based Goals**
- **Monthly Bill Target**: "Keep electricity bill under ₹2000 per month"
- **Savings Goals**: "Save ₹500 compared to previous month"
- **Cost-per-day Goals**: "Spend less than ₹50 per day on electricity"
- **Appliance-wise Budget**: Set spending limits for different appliance categories

## 3. **Behavioral Goals**
- **Usage Time Goals**: "Use AC for less than 6 hours per day"
- **Standby Elimination**: "Turn off all standby appliances before bed"
- **Smart Scheduling**: "Use washing machine only during off-peak hours"
- **Energy Audit Goals**: "Log usage for all appliances daily for 1 month"

## 4. **Environmental Goals**
- **Carbon Footprint**: "Reduce carbon footprint by 15% this quarter"
- **Green Energy**: "Use 30% renewable energy sources"
- **Efficiency Improvement**: "Upgrade to 3 energy-efficient appliances this year"

## 5. **Habit Building Goals**
- **Consistency Goals**: "Log daily usage for 30 consecutive days"
- **Monitoring Goals**: "Check energy dashboard daily"
- **Learning Goals**: "Complete 5 energy-saving tips from AI assistant"
- **Maintenance Goals**: "Schedule appliance maintenance monthly"

## 6. **Smart Home Integration Goals**
- **Automation Goals**: "Set up 5 smart home energy rules"
- **Optimization Goals**: "Achieve 90% appliance efficiency rating"
- **Real-time Monitoring**: "Monitor energy usage in real-time for 1 week"

## 7. **Competition & Social Goals**
- **Community Challenges**: "Rank in top 20% of energy savers in your area"
- **Family Goals**: "Get whole family to track usage for 1 month"
- **Seasonal Challenges**: "Reduce summer cooling costs by 25%"

## Implementation Features:
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bars and charts
- **Notifications**: Smart reminders and achievement alerts
- **Gamification**: Points, badges, and streaks for meeting goals
- **AI Recommendations**: Personalized goal suggestions based on usage patterns

## Technical Implementation:
- Goal setting interface with templates
- Progress tracking dashboard
- Notification system for goal reminders
- Achievement system with rewards
- Analytics for goal success rates
